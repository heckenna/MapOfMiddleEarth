Pelargir The_great_port_of_Gondor,_Pelargir,_is_one_of_the_first_settlements_of_the_Númenóreans_founded_in_Middle-earth_during_the_Second_Age._It_is_located_above_the_delta_of_Anduin_river_south_of_the_White_Mountains._During_the_War_of_the_Ring,_a_great_Corsair_fleet_besieged_the_city._As_Gondor's_army_was_tied_up_at_Minas_Tirith,_there_were_few_troops_to_defend_Pelargir_or_the_coasts._Then_Aragorn,_Legolas_and_Gimli,_along_with_the_Grey_Company_and_the_Army_of_the_Dead,_arrived_and_defeated_the_Corsairs._The_heroes_used_the_black_ships_to_reach_Minas_Tirith_and_defeat_Sauron's_forces.
Pinnath_Gelin The_Pinnath_Gelin_or_Green_Hills_is_a_green_and_fertile_hilled_country_in_western_Gondor_between_the_western_White_Mountains_and_Anfalas._There_are_people_who_dwell_in_the_lower_hills,_but_the_area_is_sparsely_populated.
Rivendell Rivendell,_also_known_as_Imladris,_is_an_Elven_town_and_the_house_of_Elrond_located_in_Middle-earth._The_peaceful,_sheltered_town_of_Rivendell_is_located_at_the_edge_of_a_narrow_gorge_of_the_river_Bruinen,_but_well_hidden_in_the_moorlands_and_foothills_of_the_Misty_Mountains.
Sarn_Ford Sarn_Ford_is_the_stone_ford_on_the_Brandywine_River,_on_the_far_southern_borders_of_the_Shire._It_was_guarded_by_the_Rangers_of_the_North._However,_the_Rangers_were_driven_out_of_Sarn_Ford_by_the_Ringwraiths.
Sea_of_Rhun The_Sea_of_Rhûn_is_a_large,_inland_sea_located_in_eastern_Middle-earth._During_the_War_of_the_Ring,_forces_under_the_rule_of_Sauron_mustered_in_the_lands_beyond_the_Sea_of_Rhûn._After_the_downfall_of_Sauron,_Aragorn_and_Éomer_rode_beyond_the_Sea_of_Rhûn_to_flush_out_the_last_remnants_of_the_enemy_forces_there.
Sea_of_Nurnen The_Sea_of_Núrnen,_also_called_Lake_Núrnen,_is_a_large_inland_body_of_water_in_Mordor,_Middle-earth._After_the_War_of_the_Ring,_King_Elessar_granted_the_lands_around_Núrnen_to_the_freed_slaves_of_Mordor.
Tharbad Tharbad_is_a_fortified_town_on_the_river_Greyflood,_near_the_southern_edge_of_Eriador_in_Middle-earth.
The_Wold The_Wold_also_known_as_the_Wold_of_Rohan_or_Wold_are_the_upland_grasslands_of_Rohan._This_is_where_Aragorn,_Gimli_and_Legolas_meet_Éomer_and_are_given_the_horses_Arod_and_Hasufel._During_the_War_of_the_Ring,_an_army_of_orcs_from_Dol_Guldur_unsuccessful_in_their_invasion_of_Lórien_were_surprised_by_an_attack_of_Ents_led_by_Treebeard_in_the_Wold,_and_were_defeated.
Trollshaws The_Trollshaws_are_the_upland_woods,_consisting_of_partly_beech_trees,_that_lay_to_the_west_of_Rivendell_between_the_rivers_Hoarwell_and_Loudwater_in_eastern_Eriador._Trolls_roam_these_lands,_and_terrorize_any_trespassers_that_dare_go_near_them._
Tolfolas Tolfalas_was_a_small,_rocky_island_located_in_the_Bay_of_Belfalas.
Udun Udûn_is_a_depressed_valley_located_in_northwestern_Mordor._It_lay_between_Cirith_Gorgor_and_the_Isenmouthe,_and_was_traversed_by_large_armies_of_Sauron_in_times_of_war._Within_its_circumference_are_many_Orc-holds,_including_Durthang,_the_forges,_and_the_forts_at_Carach_Angren.
Weather_Hills Weather_Hills_is_the_north-south_range_of_hills_that_lay_in_central_Eriador.
